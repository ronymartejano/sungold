About Sungold Commercial

Sungold Commercial has been a leading provider of office and school supplies since 1995. Our mission is to provide high-quality products at competitive prices with exceptional customer service.

Our Story

Founded by John and Mary Smith, Sungold Commercial began as a small shop in downtown Cityville. Over the years, we have grown to become one of the region's most trusted suppliers of office and school products.

Our Values

  • Quality - We source only the best products
  • Service - Customer satisfaction is our top priority
  • Community - We support local schools and businesses
  • Sustainability - Eco-friendly options and practices
